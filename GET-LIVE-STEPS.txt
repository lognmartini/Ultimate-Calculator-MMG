--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PART 1 — Put the code on GitHub (free file storage the cloud reads from)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

STEP 1  Open https://github.com and click "Sign up" (free).

STEP 2  After you’re logged in, click the green "New" button (new repository).

STEP 3  Name it:  mmg-calculator
        Leave everything else default.
        Click "Create repository".

STEP 4  On the new empty repo page, click "uploading an existing file".

STEP 5  Open Finder → go to your mortgage-calculator folder.
        Select ALL files and folders INSIDE it (not the parent folder).
        Drag them into the GitHub browser window.

STEP 6  Scroll down, click "Commit changes".

        ✓ Your code is now on GitHub.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PART 2 — Tell Render to run it on the internet (free hosting)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

STEP 7  Open https://render.com and click "Get Started" (free).

STEP 8  Sign up with the SAME GitHub account you just made (easiest).

STEP 9  On Render dashboard, click "New +" → "Blueprint".

STEP 10 Pick your mmg-calculator repository from the list.
         Render reads the render.yaml file automatically.
         Click "Apply" or "Deploy".

STEP 11 Wait 5–10 minutes. Status should turn green: "Live".

STEP 12 Click your service name. At the top you’ll see a URL like:
         https://mmg-calculator-xxxx.onrender.com

        THAT is your live link.
