    Quick answers

    Is this the same as a Loan Estimate?

    No. This calculator is an educational estimate. After you apply, we verify your income, assets, and full credit report and issue a federally required Loan Estimate with exact fees and cash to close.

    Will applying hurt my credit?

    We start with a soft credit pull on applications. We’ll explain if a full credit review is needed for your scenario—before you move forward.

    How is Martini different from big online lenders?

    You work with a local Senior Mortgage Strategist who designs your loan around your goals—not a call center script. You still get digital speed, plus fiduciary-style guidance and Same-As-Cash-style approval options when you qualify.

    Why does my rate change when I move the credit score slider?

    We model conventional pricing adjustments (similar to industry LLPAs) on top of today’s Freddie Mac PMMS average so your estimate reflects real-world pricing tiers—not a single generic rate.

    Do you lend outside Raleigh?

    Yes—Martini Mortgage Group helps buyers across North Carolina and can discuss other states on a case-by-case basis.

    Turn these numbers into an official quote

    This calculator gives you a strong starting point. For a formal quote with your rate, payment, and itemized closing costs, we’ll verify a few details after you apply.

    What we’ll need from you

    • Government-issued ID
    • Authorization to review credit (soft pull to start; we’ll explain full credit if needed)
    • Income documentation (pay stubs, W-2s, tax returns, or business returns if self-employed)
    • Asset statements for down payment and reserves
    • Property address, listing link, or purchase contract if you’re under contract
    • Homeowners insurance information (or we can estimate until you have a binder)

    Every file is different—employed vs. self-employed, gift funds, or multiple properties may require additional items. We’ll tell you exactly what applies to you.
